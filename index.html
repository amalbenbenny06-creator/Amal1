I Was Late... But My Love Wasn't

You waited at 12.
I didn’t show up the way you deserved.
And I’m truly sorry.

At 12, you didn’t just expect a message.
You expected effort.
You expected to feel special.

And I failed in that moment.

Not because you’re not important,
but because I didn’t realise how important that moment was to you.

I’m sorry for making you feel ignored…
especially when all you wanted was to feel loved.

If I could rewind those few minutes, I would.

But since I can’t,
let me promise you something instead —

I won’t take your feelings lightly again.

You matter.
Your expectations matter.
And I never want to be the reason you feel unimportant.

Happy Valentine’s Day… even if I said it late.

I may have missed 12,
but I don’t ever want to miss us.
